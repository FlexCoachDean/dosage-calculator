🗓️ Manual Cycle Planner

Your Oil / Compound selections are pulled in automatically as starting blocks (week 1 onward). Each block is a total mg per week for a compound, from an introduced week to an optional removed week (leave blank to run to cycle end) — spread automatically across its injection frequency. Injectables and common oral steroids are selectable. Stack blocks on the same compound to ramp up or taper. Set one cycle final week below; the graph runs 6 weeks past it to show clearance back toward baseline.
